# Plant-Growth-Monitorization
Web project for monitoring and analysis of plants' growth

Here, user need to register to do the following activities :
1) Record a planted plant
2) Record different aspects of plants like height, diameter, temperature, moisture for particular day
3) Maintain data of every day
4) Delete and update record

By obtaining and analysing this kind of data of particular plant, one can analyse the perfect requirements for growth of that plant in particular environment. So, using this, growth rate and plants' healthiness can be improved.
